Welcome to GFAST Central

Your one-stop shop providing you with easy access to online tools, technology, and support for actuarial modelling in the Cloud.

The Group Finance Actuarial Systems Team (GFAST) delivers High Performance Computing (HPC) to our global actuarial community.  

GFAST is part of Global Finance, Actuarial Hub and Actuarial Modelling & Computing.

Actuarial Cloud Environment


ACE

Actuarial Cloud Environment is Manulife’s high performance computing actuarial modelling platform used for technology enabled reporting processes.

Access to ACE is granted through access control lists (ACLs).

AXIS Enhancements


AXIS Enhancements

GFAST tracks AXIS Features & Bugs submitted by Manulife to Moody’s Analytics in a JIRA dashboard. This dashboard is updated on a biweekly basis.

For access, see these instructions.

GFAST DevOps

CHI


CHI

The Compute Hour Investigator can help identify efficient and inefficient modelling practices, improve your business processes, and reduce costs.

CSI


CSI

The Cloud Storage Investigator helps to identify storage volumes and manage overall consumption to immediately reduce storage-related expenses on the Cloud.

GMINT


GMINT

The Global Model Inventory Tool (Preview) is a repository of all Models within Manulife in a standardized format.

GMINT is used to view model lists, vetting reports, and related issues.

ACL Report


ACL Report

The AD Mail Out will cross-reference the group membership of ACLs to HR data to provide information regarding access.

To receive a monthly ACL membership report, email GFAST with your ACL list.

LANSpector


LANSpector

LANSpector crawls your unstructured data to determine who has access to your fileshares, and aid in LAN cleanup exercises.
